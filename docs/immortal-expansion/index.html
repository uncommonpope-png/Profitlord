What is Immortal Expansion?

The Concept

Immortal Expansion is the growth phase of the Profitlord ecosystem — adding specialized souls, builder capabilities, and autonomous execution loops so the Command Center can act on tasks, commit to GitHub, and evolve continuously without manual intervention.

Key Principles

Each soul is a specialized operator. Together they form a self-improving system: Scout finds opportunities, Architect designs solutions, Builder executes them, Auditor reviews quality, and Scribe documents everything.
